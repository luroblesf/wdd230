Overview

Purpose

We are a business organization that leads the defense, representation and promotion of trade; seeking all companies in Quetzaltenango to be part of this institution. We believe in our institutional principles, free enterprise and the rule of law; We strive to adequately and timely comply with all the commitments we assume in order to effectively satisfy the needs of our clients.
